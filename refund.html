Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025
🛡 Our Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 24 hours of purchase, no questions asked. If the programme does not meet your expectations, email us and we will return every rupee.

1. Eligibility for a Refund

You are eligible for a full refund if you submit your request within 24 hours of your original purchase date. There is no minimum content engagement required — if you want a refund within this window, you will receive one.

2. How to Request a Refund

Email us at Persuasionacademybyra@gmail.com with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • The email address used at the time of purchase
  • Your order/transaction ID (available in your purchase confirmation email)

You do not need to provide a reason, though feedback is always welcome.

3. Processing Time

We will acknowledge your refund request within 1 business day. Once approved, refunds are processed back to the original payment method. Razorpay typically completes refunds within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

4. After a Refund is Issued

Upon completion of a refund, your access to the programme will be deactivated. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

5. Requests Outside the Refund Window

Refund requests made after the 24-hour window cannot be honoured as a matter of standard policy. If you are experiencing a technical issue preventing access to your purchased content, please contact us at Persuasionacademybyra@gmail.com and we will work to resolve it promptly.
